The Data Dilemma

The world is generating more data than ever before — but storing it the traditional way is hurting our planet.

⚡

Massive Energy Consumption

Traditional data centers require huge amounts of electricity for cooling and running machines.

🏙️

Urban Overcrowding

They take up valuable city space and add to infrastructure stress and costs.

🌍

Environmental Harm

From carbon emissions to e-waste, data centers are major contributors to pollution.

Our Solution

Stratobyte brings innovation to the skies with its unique approach to sustainable data storage.

🌍 Eco-Friendly

Runs entirely on solar energy with minimal carbon footprint.

☁️ Stratospheric Pods

Stores data high above in the stratosphere, eliminating land use and cooling needs.

📶 Seamless Connectivity

Connected via satellite uplinks ensuring uninterrupted access to data.
